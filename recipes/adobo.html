Adobo

Descrioption

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish that involves meat, seafood, or vegetables marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and black peppercorns, which is browned in oil, and simmered in the marinade. It has a strong savory flavor and is often served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 pound chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Add the chicken and marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until soft.
  3. Add the chicken and marinade to the skillet and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet. Boil the remaining sauce until it thickens.
  5. Pour the sauce over the chicken and serve with steamed rice.
